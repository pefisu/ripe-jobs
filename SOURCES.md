## Fit Rules For Broader Roles

Strong fits:

- international patient coordinator, medical tourism specialist, health tourism sales, clinic coordinator, clinic manager
- English required, Turkish useful
- Istanbul European side, hybrid, or remote
- CRM, reporting, pricing, sales, patient follow-up, clinic operations, or team leadership

Good fits:

- broader healthcare coordinator / sales roles
- customer success or account management roles where English + CRM + sales matter
- remote/hybrid roles that are not healthcare but use her international-patient/sales background

Maybe fits:

- pure interpreter roles below her level
- roles requiring Russian/German/Arabic when English is not enough
- far commute with no hybrid option
- generic call-center roles with little coordination or growth
